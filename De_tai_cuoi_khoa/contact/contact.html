Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Reservations

How do I make a booking?

You can make a booking by browsing our destinations, selecting your preferred travel package, and following the checkout process. You can also call our customer service team for assistance.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 24 hours before departure. Changes may be subject to availability and additional fees. Contact our support team for assistance.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before departure for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in transparent pricing. All fees including taxes and service charges are clearly displayed during the booking process. No hidden fees will be added later.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 10 or more people. Contact our group sales team for customized quotes and special arrangements.

Travel & Support

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, travel insurance is included in all our packages. It covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost luggage. Additional coverage options are available.

What if I need help during my trip?

Our 24/7 customer support team is always available to help you during your trip. You can reach us via phone, email, or live chat from anywhere in the world.

Do you offer multi-language support?

Yes, our customer service team speaks multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, and Mandarin. We'll connect you with a representative who speaks your language.
